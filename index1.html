Free Movie Watching Website

How to Use:
  • Enter the IMDb code of the movie from your browser you want to watch in the input field. (For example: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt13622970/ enter tt13622970 in the input field)
  • Click on the "Load Movie" button to load the movie.
  • Switch between dark and light themes for your comfort using the theme selector.
